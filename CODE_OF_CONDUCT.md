# Code of conduct

`rules_ostest` is committed to a respectful, welcoming, and productive
community.

## Expected behavior

- Be considerate, patient, and constructive.
- Discuss ideas and technical tradeoffs without personal attacks.
- Welcome people with different backgrounds and levels of experience.
- Respect privacy and stop unwanted contact when asked.
- Accept responsibility, apologize, and repair harm when mistakes happen.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, deliberate
intimidation, doxxing, and sustained disruption are not acceptable in project
spaces or when representing the project elsewhere.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to a maintainer through the repository host's
private contact or moderation features. If a concern involves a maintainer, use
the hosting provider's abuse-reporting channel. Do not open a public issue that
identifies a target of harassment.

Maintainers may edit or remove content and may warn, temporarily suspend, or
permanently exclude participants when needed to protect the community. Reports
will be handled as confidentially as practical, with attention to the safety and
preferences of the person affected.
